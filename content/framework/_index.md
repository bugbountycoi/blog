The Bug Bounty Community of Interest (BB COI) has developed a comprehensive framework to help organizations navigate the complex world of bug bounty programs. This framework provides practical guidance, best practices, and strategic insights for organizations at any stage of their bug bounty journey.

Our framework is designed as a progressive guide, taking you from initial considerations through implementation and optimization of your bug bounty program. Each chapter builds upon the previous one, creating a complete roadmap for success.

### Getting Started

If you're new to bug bounties, we recommend starting with the [announcement](announcing-the-bug-bounty-framework/) and [Chapter 1](chapter-1/) to build your foundational understanding. For those evaluating whether a bug bounty program is right for their organization, jump directly to [Chapter 2](chapter-2/).

Each chapter is designed to be both standalone reference material and part of a comprehensive learning journey. Take what's relevant to your situation and use it to build a program that fits your organization's unique needs and culture.
